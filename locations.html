Heritage & Tourism Zone

Orchha

One of the most scenic and historically significant towns near Jhansi, Orchha is gaining attention from investors and buyers looking for weekend homes and tourism-adjacent property. Located just 18 km from Jhansi, it offers excellent value for plots and residential units in a heritage setting.

High appreciation potential — tourism-driven Plots available at competitive rates Proximity to Orchha Fort and Betwa River Ideal for second homes and investment

Investment Corridor

Lalitpur

A rapidly developing district adjacent to Jhansi, Lalitpur offers affordable plots and builder floors for first-time buyers and investors. With NH-44 running through the area and ongoing infrastructure development, it represents a strong mid-term investment opportunity in the Bundelkhand region.

Affordable entry prices — strong upside potential NH-44 frontage plots available Industrial zone proximity driving demand Suitable for long-term investment buyers
